Matrices and other arrays

Try this: 

			a <- matrix(1:4, 2, 2)
			a[3]
			a[1,2]
			a[3] <- 100
			b <- as.character(a)
			d <- a
			d[1,2] = "test"

			e[1,2] <- NA
			is.na(e)
